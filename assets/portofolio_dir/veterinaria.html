Description

Veterinaria Santa Rosa de Lima is an open source project that he developed for a veterinarian with a beautiful purpose: to provide free treatments to animals. This application is designed to help the veterinarian to efficiently manage its client base and offer detailed follow-up of each pet.
One of the main features of Veterinaria Santa Rosa de Lima is its ability to add and remove clients. Users can easily register pet owners and store their information such as name, address and contact details. This allows for an organized management of the veterinary client base.
In addition to adding clients, the application allows you to authorize pets for each registered client. This makes it easy to identify and track pets at the vet. Each pet can be associated with relevant information such as species, breed, date of birth, medical history, and any other important data for their care.
Santa Rosa de Lima Veterinary Clinic also includes a diagnostic function, where the vet can add information about the health status of each pet. This includes fields to enter characteristics, diseases, and a basic conclusion from the veterinarian. This feature helps maintain an up-to-date medical history and provides an overview of each pet's condition.
The app features a search panel that allows users to find customers and see which pets are associated with their name. This streamlines the process of finding specific information and makes it easy to track each pet at the vet.
Veterinaria Santa Rosa de Lima is an open source project that reflects my commitment to helping animals and my passion for programming. By including this project in my portfolio, I want to highlight my ability to develop technological solutions that have a positive impact on society and support important causes.
I am proud to have developed Veterinaria Santa Rosa de Lima, and I hope that its inclusion in my portfolio shows my dedication to creating practical applications that benefit animals and the community at large.
