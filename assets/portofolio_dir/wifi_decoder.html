Description

Wifi QR is an application that I have developed and that is part of my programming portfolio. This application has the functionality to decode QR codes of Wi-Fi networks, which allows users to see the password and store it in a local database. One of the main features of Wifi QR is its ability to import images that contain QR codes from Wi-Fi networks. Users can upload an image from their device and the app will automatically scan the QR code, extracting the Wi-Fi network information, including the password. In addition, Wifi QR also offers the option to directly scan QR codes using the device's camera. This makes it easy to quickly get Wi-Fi network information without the need to import images. The application has been designed with an intuitive and easy-to-use interface. Once the QR code of the Wi-Fi network has been decoded, users can view and save the password in a local database. This provides the convenience of having quick access to previously scanned Wi-Fi network passwords, which can be especially useful in situations where you need to log into different networks frequently. By including Wifi QR in my portfolio, I want to highlight my ability to develop applications that simplify password management for Wi-Fi networks and improve the user experience when accessing these networks. This app demonstrates my knowledge in image processing, QR code decoding and data storage. I am proud to have developed Wifi QR as part of my portfolio, and I am confident that its inclusion demonstrates my ability to create practical and useful solutions in the field of mobile applications. Wifi QR is a tangible example of my passion for programming and my ability to develop innovative and functional applications. I am excited to feature this app in my portfolio and highlight my experience in mobile app development.
